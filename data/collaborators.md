## Benoît Guillod
title: Senior Researcher
affiliation: HES-SO Valais
website: https://www.hevs.ch

Benoît works on climate-impact modelling and regional hydrology, with a focus on
probabilistic forecasting of extreme weather events. He brings expertise in
large-ensemble climate simulations and their integration into machine learning pipelines.

## Sylvain Lobry
title: Associate Professor
affiliation: Université Paris Cité
website: https://www.lobry.xyz

Sylvain is an expert in deep learning for remote sensing and geospatial data. His
research spans semantic segmentation of satellite imagery and self-supervised
